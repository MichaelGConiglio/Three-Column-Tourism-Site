Enjoy Your Stay In Fort Lauderdale

If you are a lover of sun and beautiful clear blue water, enjoy 23 miles of pristine beaches, and pick from one of Fort Lauderdale's fourteen beaches.

Why just swim in the ocean when you can enjoy one of our many watersports in Fort Lauderdale? Catch a ride on a banana boat, fly on a water powered jetpacked, zoom around on a jetski, or pick an amazing thing out of a multitude!

When it gets dark out, why let the fun of the day end? Keep celebrating at one of Fort Lauderdale's many nationally renowned danceclubs. A place where many memories can be made as well as new friends!
